    POSEIDON Surf and Dive is a locally built and run website in which details about both surf and dive conditions around Tasmania are updated each week. From recommendations to warnings, POSEIDON can let you know.


    Updates can include: water temperature, wind speed and direction, synoptic charts, as well as swell direction, size and period.


    Click either the Surf or Dive option on the pages banner to see the conditions for yourself!
